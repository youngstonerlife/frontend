Welcome!

Paws & Hope is a non-profit animal shelter dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and stray animals. We believe every pet deserves love, care, and a forever home.



About our shelter

At Paws & Hope, we rescue, care for, and rehome abandoned and mistreated animals. Our mission is simple: give every pet a second chance at love and life. With your support, we’ve already helped over 500 animals find their forever homes.
